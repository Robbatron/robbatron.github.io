ABOUT THE PROJECT

Angular Kitchen Sink is everything but AND the kitchen sink! I have teamed up with a highly knowledgeable co-worker to create an Angular templating and scaffolding engine that uses AngularJS best practice (as communicated by Todd Motto and John Papa) to generate templates with Node.js and custom Gulp tasks. With this side-project we hope to enable new hires to jump right in to coding vanilla JS without needing advanced knowledge of AngularJS.
