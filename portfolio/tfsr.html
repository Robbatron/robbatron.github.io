tfs rollups report generator

ABOUT THE PROJECT

TFS Rollups was another C#, XAML, MVC, JavaScript side-project that I and a coworker decided to undertake in-between important projects. Our goal was to make management happy by auto-generating reports for them by pulling data from Team Foundation Server and displaying it in charts and graphs that they could then analyze and display at board meetings. This tool saved us and management thousands of hours and the code was really fun to write both on the OS and the browser side!
