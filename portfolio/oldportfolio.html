ABOUT THE PROJECT

I am including this here for the memories. I built this site from the ground-up with JavaScript and your run-of-the-mill HTML & CSS just as something to put on the top of my resume when applying for jobs on campus. The screen mimicked a CLI and the text flew across the screen as if it were being typed by a fiercely experienced coder, and all it took was a JavaScript file that processed text from a text file! I have since graduated to making enterprise websites with advanced technology stacks such as MEAN & LAMP, and while this site is horribly structured, I occasionally go back to it to put things into perspective when I feel like I am over-complicating things.

 

It even had a hidden snake game!
